History: Our managing director "Mr. Benjamin Biswas" start this company at 31-07-2012 by purchasing biswasit.com domain. He plan to provide service to his local & International freelancing marketplace customer through this website only for website design service. But later 2014, when he saw his customer are facing problem to getting domain, hosting & mainly local customer are facing problem with domain & hosting purchase with $ currency because most of them don't have PayPal or Dual Currency Credit card. Then he plan to sell domain, hosting, VPS & dedicated server. So, we can said our company start domain, hosting business from 2014. Still we don't have any license because it was a test run, we don't know that we can survive in this business. After 2 years later when he saw now we have enough client's to survive then he plan to do Trade License & 18.08.2016 we got our Trade License from City Corporation & our name was "Biswas Information & Technology Solutions" or short name "BiswasIT" . After getting our Trade License we become e-Cab Member few times later. Until 04-02-2020 we run our business with same name & License. In the mean time we connected with 2checkout as a Merchant & start accepting PayPal & Credit or Debit card payment. We also connected with SSLCOMMERZ & start accepting Bkash, Rocket & Card Online Payment. We become CloudLinux partner, 12 domain registrar company partner & 10 world class hosting company partner. When our business grow day by day by the love of our client's Mr. Benjamin Biswas plan to register our company as a Limited company from Registrar of Joint Stock Companies And Firms (RJSC) & 04.02.2020 we become register as a Limited Company with a new name "Biswas Host Ltd." After getting new license we changed our main business domain biswasit.com to biswashost.com . Our current business 1st domain is biswashost.com & 2nd domain is biswasit.com . So this is the short history of "Biswas Host Ltd." or "BiswasIT" & now we provide service to the Worldwide.
